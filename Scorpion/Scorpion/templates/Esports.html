What is Esports?

Esports is a global gaming league made up of semi-pro and pro teams that compete in tournaments for prizes

Pro Regions: NA(North America), EU(Europe), ROC (China), Korea, Oceanic

Counter Strike: Global Offensive

CS:GO is a multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Valve. This game is the fourth installment in the Counter Strike series and was released to Windows, OS X, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3 on August 21st, 2012.

Dota 2

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Valve. The game is a sequel to Defense of the Ancients, which was a community-created mod for Blizzard Entertainment's Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos and its expansion pack, The Frozen Throne. It was released to Windows on July 9th, 2013.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena esports video game developed and published by Riot Games for Microsoft Windows and macOS. Originally inspired by Defense of the Ancients, the game follows a freemium model and is commonly described as the world's most iconic esport. It was released on October 27th, 2009.
